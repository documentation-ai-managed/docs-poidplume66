## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: API access if you plan to upload documents programmatically

</Callout>

## Step 1: Register Your Account

Sign up for poidplume66 in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com` and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link from poidplume66 and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your project docs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Documentation Space**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like `MyProjectDocs`, add a description, and choose visibility (public or private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create**. You'll land in your new space's editor.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Upload Initial Documents

Add your first files using the method that suits you.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="upload">
    Open the **Files** panel on the left sidebar.
    
    Drag your documents (PDFs, MDX, images) directly into the upload zone.
    
    Supported formats: `.mdx`, `.md`, `.pdf`, `.png`, `.jpg` up to `50MB`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="API Upload" icon="code">
    Use the poidplume66 API for programmatic uploads.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
      ````javascript
      const formData = new FormData();
      formData.append('file', yourFile);
      formData.append('spaceId', 'your-space-id');

      const response = await fetch('https://api.example.com/v1/spaces/{spaceId}/upload', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: {
          'Authorization': `Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`
        },
        body: formData
      });
      ````
      ````bash
      curl -X POST https://api.example.com/v1/spaces/{spaceId}/upload \
        -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
        -F "file=@/path/to/document.mdx"
      ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  After uploading, documents auto-index for search. Use tags like `api`, `guide` for better organization.
</Callout>
