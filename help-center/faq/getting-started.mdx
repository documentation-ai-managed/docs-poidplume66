## Create Your First Project

To start documenting with poidplume66:

1. Log into your poidplume66 account.
2. From the dashboard, click "New Project".
3. Enter a project name and brief description.
4. Click "Create".
5. Your project will appear on the homepage for easy access.

Tip: You can add collaborators from the project settings.

## Next Step

Begin adding or importing documents to your project workspace.
