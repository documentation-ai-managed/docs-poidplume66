## Arrange Docs for Clarity

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add folders">
    Click "Add Folder" in your project sidebar to group related docs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move documents">
    Drag and drop docs into folders for better organization.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Rename items">
    Click the item name to rename documents or folders for easier navigation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share structure">
    Invite teammates to your project to collaborate on the organized workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
